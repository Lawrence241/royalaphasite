Who We are

We are committed to providing a responsive and reliable service

Incorporated in February 2013, Royal Alpha Energy was established primarily for the purpose of deploying various waste management techniques to combat the problem of increasing waste generation in our society. Our services are of industry best practices to generate products of high quality using the most effective and efficient methods. We are in partnership with international experts with great repute in Africa, Europe and Asia whose operations have recorded huge success with this method of waste management.
Mission statement Creating a cleaner, greener and healthier environment
Vision statement To be a world class provider of renewable energy
