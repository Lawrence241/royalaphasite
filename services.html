Waste Collection and Recycling Services

Waste Management is not the core competence of your firm or municipality. We at Royalapha Energy apply the highest safety and environmental standards to the treatment of solid waste. For you it is often time-consuming and costly, not to mention exposing your company to major environmental and reputational risks. Our customers have peace of mind that partnering with us allows them to save money and focus on the critical parts of their business and customers.

Environmental Sustainability

We know Environmental Sustainability. Providing a clean environment for people and business, not only increases attractiveness as living space but also for business investment. We live sustainability daily working to achieve Zero Waste to Landfill, through recycling, reusing and reducing.

Land Fill Management Services

Our Materials Recovery Facility (MRF) is where the valuable fraction collected from municipal solid waste (MSW) is sorted into its different types (e.g. plastics, cardboard, paper, metal) using a mixture of manual and automated processes. When the materials have been sorted they are sent to reprocessors and manufacturers where they are used to create new products. Please contact us to product and latest prices.

Consultancy

Managing, protecting and restoring the environment are core to our business. If you require, planning, design and construction, turnkey development, environmental impact assessments or more holistic approach to achieving your social responsibility requirements, We are uniquely placed to help you.

Recyclable Mining Services

We focus on extracting valuable resources and materials from waste, whether it's electronic waste, construction waste, or other recyclable materials and process valuable materials for reuse. This involve services that help businesses or industries source recyclable materials to meet their sustainability goals. These services may involve identifying reliable suppliers of recyclable materials or creating networks to connect waste generators with recyclers.
