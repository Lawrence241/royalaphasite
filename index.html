Welcome to Royal Alpha Energy

Royal Alpha Energy is dedicated to developing and embedding the sustainability of economies. We currently operate at the Saje dumpsite in Abeokuta, Ogun state where we recover the recyclable portions from the Municipal Solid Waste. This is then sent back to recyclers for re-use as feedstock for plastic, metal and paper recycling.

With the aid of black soldier fly, we are able to convert the organic component of the MSW to animal feeds protein. Our key focus is diverting waste from landfills, reducing greenhouse gas emission, and serving the communities within which we operate.
